Reliability is paramount, and it’s where technicians play a vital role. Data centers are classified into four tiers based on their redundancy.

A Tier 1 facility is the most basic, with single points of failure.

Tier 2 adds some redundant components for more resilience.

Tier 3 is where the role of a technician shines. It’s concurrently maintainable, meaning you can perform maintenance—like swapping a power supply—without service interruption. This provides 99.982% availability.

Finally, Tier 4 is fully fault-tolerant. As a technician here, you are maintaining a near-perfectly resilient environment with only minutes of potential downtime per year.
