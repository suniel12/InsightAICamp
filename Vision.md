Current education is fundamentally unprepared for the gigawatt era:

- **Electrical Domain**: Industrial trainers understand substations but lack data center context

- **Compute Domain**: Proprietary manufacturer "walled gardens" creating siloed knowledge
- **Academic Pipeline**: Traditional IT focus, unprepared for electro-mechanical realities

**Result**: Companies forced to act as training general contractors, piecing together disjointed curriculum that fails to create system-level thinkers.
